Decline Analysis

Top Decline Reasons

Critical Opportunity
33% of all declines are due to "CBR ID Verify Decline" – identity verification failures. An additional 25% are due to "Unverified SSN" issues. Combined, these two categories represent 58% of all declines and are prime targets for Socure's enhanced verification.

Breakdown by Category

  1. CBR ID Verify Decline (33%) – Identity verification failures, address/name mismatches
  2. Unverified SSN (25%) – SSN validation failures, verification issues
  3. Fraud Indicators (18%) – Fraud-related decline codes, risk signals
  4. Credit Issues (15%) – FICO floors, credit bureau declines
  5. Other Reasons (9%) – Misc. operational and policy codes
